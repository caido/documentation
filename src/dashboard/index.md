# Dashboard

The Caido Dashboard is a web application hosted at [https://dashboard.caido.io](https://dashboard.caido.io) that provides a centralized interface for managing your Caido account and related services.

## What you can do

Through the Dashboard, you can:

- **Manage your account**: Update your profile information, preferences, and account settings
- **Handle billing**: View and manage your subscription, payment methods, and billing history
- **Organize teams**: Create and manage teams, invite members, and configure team settings and permissions

The Dashboard serves as the administrative hub for your Caido experience, allowing you to control all aspects of your account and subscription from a single, easy-to-use web interface.
