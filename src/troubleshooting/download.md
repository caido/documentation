# Download Issues

## "This site can't be reached"

This error may occur when your IP address has been blocked by the storage provider's firewall.

<code><Icon icon="fas fa-screwdriver-wrench" /></code> If you encounter this error message after attempting to download Caido use a VPN or change your DNS provider and then refresh the page.
